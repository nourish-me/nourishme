Why NourishMe exists

Three months ago I had twins. During pregnancy and the first weeks after the birth I noticed how much our own nutrition matters — first for the kids of course, but ultimately also for us moms ourselves. When breastfeeding, every nutrient goes to the babies first. Then to me. If I don't actively pay attention, it comes from my reserves. Tiredness, brittle nails, hair loss — these are often real deficiency symptoms, not just "mom life".

I wanted to track this. But existing apps simply aren't built for it — not for pregnant women, not for moms with one baby, not for moms with multiples, not for breastfeeding, not for exclusive pumping. None told me concretely what to eat next. None backed up their recommendations with sources.

So I started building myself a Coach — evidence-based, adapted to my exact situation, with live coaching after every meal. It helped me — and I built it so it works for any mom in this phase, whether with one baby or multiples, pregnant or postpartum, breastfeeding directly or pumping exclusively.

— Vanessa

Three things NourishMe does differently

1. Live coaching, not just tracking

After every meal you get an immediate concrete hint: "You still need 30 g of protein today, a salmon bowl at lunch would be a good fit." No more mental math, no more Googling.

2. Calculated for your exact situation

Trimester, number of children being nursed, exclusive pumping or direct nursing, your actual daily milk volume — all individual. Twins exclusively breastfed? +1,260 kcal, not the generic "+500" default.

3. Science-transparent, your data stays with you

Every recommendation is backed by a cited source: DGE 2025, BfR, EFSA, LactMed (NIH), FDA/EPA. Tap the ⓘ to read it. No account, no advertising tracking, no cloud. Everything stays on your device.

How it works

  1. 3-minute onboarding Trimester, height, weight, activity, number of children, estimated milk volume. NourishMe calculates your daily energy and macro needs from those.
  2. Log meals by text or photo "Cereal with yogurt, one bowl" — done. The coach parses nutrients and portion size. For photos it auto-detects the content.
  3. Get a coach hint immediately Every meal gets a concrete suggestion for what to eat next — based on your today's progress and your individual daily target.
  4. Food-safety built in Caffeine thresholds, alcohol wait times, mercury fish lists, listeria risks — all from official sources.

Scientific basis

All thresholds and recommendations in NourishMe are based on:

  • German Nutrition Society (DGE 2025)
  • German Federal Institute for Risk Assessment (BfR)
  • European Food Safety Authority (EFSA)
  • LactMed / National Institutes of Health (NIH)
  • U.S. Food and Drug Administration / EPA
  • WHO Antenatal Care Guidelines

NourishMe is not a medical device. It does not replace medical or midwife advice. For medical questions, consult your physician or midwife.
