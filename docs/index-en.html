Why NourishMe exists

14 weeks ago I was in bed, two babies on my chest, the hunger of a small wolf pack in my head.

What I needed: an app that tells me what I need today, without making me track. What I found: calorie counters built for bodybuilders. Pregnancy apps that ended the day of birth. Breastfeeding apps that added a flat „+500 kcal", regardless of one baby or twins.

So I started building. Grounded in DGE, BfR, EFSA and LactMed. Calculated for my exact situation. With a coach that says, after every meal, what would make sense next. It helped me, and it's built to fit every mom in this phase.

— Vanessa

Three things that really differ

Coach, not counter

After every meal a concrete hint: „You still need 30 g of protein today. A salmon bowl at lunch would fit." No mental math, no Googling.

Calculated for you, not averages

Trimester, children being nursed, exclusive pumping, your actual milk volume. Twins exclusively breastfed? +1,260 kcal, not „+500".

Science open, data with you

Every recommendation has a source: DGE 2025, BfR, EFSA, LactMed, FDA/EPA. One tap shows it. No account, no cloud sync, everything stays local.

How it works

  1. Three-minute onboarding Trimester, height, weight, activity, number of children, estimated milk volume. NourishMe derives your energy and macro needs from those.
  2. Log meals by text or photo „Cereal with yogurt, one bowl." Done. The coach parses nutrients and portion size. For photos it auto-detects the content.
  3. Get a coach hint immediately Per meal, one concrete suggestion for what's next, based on today's progress and your individual daily target.
  4. Food-safety built in Caffeine, alcohol wait times, mercury fish lists, listeria risks. All from official sources, no searching required.
