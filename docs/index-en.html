Why NourishMe exists

14 weeks ago I was in bed, postpartum, two babies on my chest, a hunger in my head that wasn't only mine. But it had started already during pregnancy.

From the first trimester it was clear: I was nourishing more than just me. Every nutrient goes to the little ones first, through the placenta during pregnancy, through milk afterwards. I have to be well-supplied myself so the whole system runs, and „skipping a meal" isn't really an option. What I needed: an app that tells me live what nourishes all of us. What I found: calorie counters built for weight-loss. Pregnancy apps that ended the day of birth. Breastfeeding apps with a flat „+500 kcal", regardless of one baby or twins.

So I started building. Grounded in DGE, BfR, EFSA and LactMed. Calculated for my exact situation. With a coach that says, after every meal, what would make sense next. It helped me, and it's built to fit every mom and mom-to-be in this phase.

— Vanessa

Three things that really differ

Real-time nourishment

You're feeding more than yourself in this phase. After every meal a concrete hint of what keeps you and your little ones well-supplied: „You still need 30 g of protein today, a salmon bowl at lunch would fit." No mental math, no Googling.

Calculated for you, not averages

Trimester, children being nursed, exclusive pumping, your actual milk volume. Twins exclusively breastfed? +1,260 kcal, not „+500".

Science open, data with you

Every recommendation has a source: DGE 2025, BfR, EFSA, LactMed, FDA/EPA. One tap shows it. No account, no cloud sync, everything stays local.

How it works

  1. Three-minute onboarding Trimester, height, weight, activity, number of children, estimated milk volume. NourishMe derives your energy and macro needs from those.
  2. Log meals by text or photo „Cereal with yogurt, one bowl." Done. The coach parses nutrients and portion size. For photos it auto-detects the content.
  3. Get a coach hint immediately Per meal, one concrete suggestion for what's next, based on today's progress and your individual daily target.
  4. Food-safety built in Caffeine, alcohol wait times, mercury fish lists, listeria risks. All from official sources, no searching required.
